Who I Am

I'm a Master's student at Northeastern University pursuing Artificial Intelligence, bringing 2+ years of hands-on experience in machine learning, computer vision and AI system development along with expertise in full-stack development and cross-functional project leadership.

I'm seeking opportunities in AI/ML engineering, computer vision or AI research where I can leverage my deep learning background to develop innovative solutions that combine technical excellence with meaningful real-world impact.
